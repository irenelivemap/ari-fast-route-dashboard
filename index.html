What to prioritize next

ARI Fast is broadly competitive with Google, so the next wins are about making fast routes feel more direct, easier to follow, and better to walk when similarly fast alternatives exist.

Fix missed shortcutsCatch obvious cut-throughs, pedestrian paths, parks, bridges, and local shortcuts.
Simplify the routeAvoid unnecessary detours and too many turns when speed is similar.
Avoid busy streetsUse calmer fast alternatives when available.
Prefer nicer fast pathsFavor river, lake, park, or pedestrian routes when time is comparable.
